# Brain Score

A numeric score summarising a user's brain-training progress, agreed off the back of User Research
Finding 2.

**Current: agreed but unimplemented.** The decision to add a numeric Brain Score was taken, but
the current design does not show one.

## The gap (J)
- **Decision (Research Finding 2):** add a numeric Brain Score.
- **Design Screen 4 — Progress (2026-05-10):** shows "Brain Athlete — Level 4," a weekly-activity
  bar chart, a Brain Skills radar chart, and an achievements badge grid — but **no numeric score
  anywhere on the screen.**

## Open issues
- Design owes an agreed decision. **Needs a design update to add the score, or a recorded decision
  to drop it.**
