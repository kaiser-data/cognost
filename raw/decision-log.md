# BrainFlow Decision Log

Append-only. Each entry: `## [date] decision | topic`.

## [2026-02-14] decision | Paywall timing
In the growth review, Marketing (Sven Albrecht) argued that a Day-7 paywall delays revenue
and that moving the upgrade prompt to **Day 3** would lift the free-to-premium conversion
toward the 8% target. **Decision: move the premium upgrade prompt to Day 3.** Owner: Marketing.

## [2026-03-11] discussion | Paywall pushback
UX (Mara Köhler) and Product flagged that User Research Finding 3 shows users only convert
*after* an experienced streak, and that a Day-3 prompt risks early churn. No decision taken;
escalated to the product review.

## [2026-04-09] decision | Paywall timing (revisit)
Product review concluded the research evidence outweighs the short-term revenue argument.
**Decision: revert the premium upgrade prompt to Day 7.** This supersedes the 2026-02-14
decision. Owner: Product.

## [2026-05-06] decision | AI Daily Pick scope
PM proposed pulling the **AI Daily Pick** into the MVP because User Research Finding 1 shows
choice overload kills first-session activation. Engineering (Tomáš Novák) flagged that this
was a "Won't have" item in the PRD and warned it adds material scope to the Q2 launch.
**Decision (PM): include AI Daily Pick in the MVP.** Engineering recorded a scope-risk
objection.

## [2026-05-19] open question | Health-data DPIA
Do we need a formal GDPR Data Protection Impact Assessment before the health-insurer pilots?
No owner assigned. Unresolved.
