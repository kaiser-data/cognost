01 / Skill development

It learns its own rules from its own mistakes

The brain doesn't get smarter by reading more. It scores its own answers against a rubric — did it cite a source, surface the disagreement, say which value is current? — and for every dimension that failed, it adopts the operating rule that would have fixed it, lifted straight from the maintainer contract. Proposed, then applied explicitly.

Baseline skill · v1

"You answer questions about the BrainFlow product using the knowledge base.

— Be helpful and concise.
— Give the user a direct answer."

Diagnosis over the scored run: cite, conflict and currency dimensions all failed. The answers sounded certain and hid the disagreement.

→

Maintainer skill · learned

Provenance is mandatory CLAUDE.md §3.1

Every claim cites its source document and date, or it isn't stated.

Preserve contradictions CLAUDE.md §3.2

When sources disagree, present both with provenance. Never pick a winner by guessing.

State current vs. superseded CLAUDE.md §3.6

Say what's true now, what it replaced, and the date. Never serve a stale fact as live.
