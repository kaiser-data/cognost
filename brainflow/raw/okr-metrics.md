# BrainFlow OKRs & Metric Definitions

## North-star metric
**7-day retention ≥ 40%** — the share of users who reopen the app within 7 days of install.
This is the single headline number we steer the company by.

## Supporting KPIs
| KPI | Target | Warning | Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free-to-Premium conversion | ≥ 8% within 30 days | < 4% | Monthly |
| Customer Acquisition Cost (B2C) | ≤ €15 | > €25 | Monthly per channel |
| CLV : CAC ratio | ≥ 3 : 1 | < 2 : 1 | Quarterly |

## Notes
The hypothesis behind the product is that AI-personalised micro-interventions raise 7-day
retention to 40%. Marketing reports retention as a single "40% retention" figure in external
decks.
