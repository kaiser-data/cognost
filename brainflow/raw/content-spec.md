# BrainFlow Exercise Content Specification

## Exercise format
Each guided exercise runs **3–10 minutes** depending on the category and difficulty level.
Focus and Emotional IQ sessions tend toward the longer end (up to 10 minutes) because they
require a settling-in period; Wellbeing breathers can be as short as 3 minutes.

Every exercise includes a **"The Science"** panel explaining the neurological benefit
(e.g. prefrontal-cortex activation) to reinforce evidence-based trust.

## Categories
Focus, Creativity, Emotional IQ, Wellbeing.

## Content sourcing
All content is authored or reviewed by neuroscientists and psychologists.
