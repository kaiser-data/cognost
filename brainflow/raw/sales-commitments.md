# B2B Pilot Commitment — Müller GmbH

Müller GmbH (120 employees) signed a 12-month pilot. To close the deal, Sales committed to the
following, confirmed in the signed order form:

1. **Per-employee engagement dashboard** — the HR team will see individual employees' usage,
   streaks, and category breakdowns by name, so they can "follow up with low-engagement staff."
2. **Price: €22 per user / year** — a negotiated rate to win the logo.
3. **Offline mode** — exercises must work without a network connection, because some staff
   work on the factory floor with poor connectivity.
4. Go-live: **Q2 2026**, alongside the consumer launch.
